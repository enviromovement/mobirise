Liana Dela Cruz graduated from University of the Philippines, with a degree in Architecture in 2023. Her architectural vocabulary evolved to design pure imposing geometric forms. Brick is a key material in her poetics, used as a covering material to underline the severity of the volumes she designs.

Every one of her works is coabroadvered with a mantle of sacredness, through which she demonstrates the importance of architecture as a means of expression of human memory.

In 2024, Liana opened her own practice in New york and began a busy career designing highly significant, exemplary works of architecture, including several prominent religious buildings.
Around the same time she began her teaching and research career, holding conferences, seminars and courses in Italy and abroad
